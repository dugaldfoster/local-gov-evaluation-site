## What this hub is for
A practical library to support evaluation in councils and place-based partnerships — from quick process learning to stronger impact designs.

## A simple evaluation path
1. Clarify the decision and timeframe  
2. Map outcomes and assumptions  
3. Choose design and comparison strategy  
4. Plan data, ethics, and governance  
5. Analyse and communicate clearly  
6. Turn findings into actions
